Global Dual Degree Programs

Chitkara University's Global Dual Degree Programs, in collaboration with top institutions in the US, Canada, and Australia, provide students with world-class academic mentorship and international exposure, enabling them to earn degrees from both Chitkara University and prestigious global universities.




Bachelor of Business Administration

Earn a Business Administration degree from Trent University in Ontario, Canada. Academic mentorship program allows BBA students at Chitkara University to earn 100% credits and graduate with a Trent BBA degree in Canada.

B.E. in Computer Science & Technology

Enroll in Chitkara University's B.E. in Computer Science and Technology. Study for the first 2 years in India and transfer to Arizona State University for 3rd & 4th years to obtain a prestigious Computer Science degree from ASU.

Bachelor of Engineering

Experience world-class education with our dual degree program. Complete initial years at Chitkara and finish your degree at our prestigious international partner university.

Bachelor of Information Technology

Partner with Deakin University, Australia. Study for the first 2 years at Chitkara University and complete your final years at Deakin University to earn a globally recognized IT degree from one of Australia's top universities.

Master of Business Administration

Advanced MBA program with international exposure. Begin your studies at Chitkara and complete specialization at our partner university for enhanced career prospects.

Bachelor of Science

Comprehensive science education with global perspective. Start at Chitkara University and transition to our international partner for advanced studies and research opportunities.

Bachelor of Arts

Liberal arts education with international dimension. Experience diverse academic culture by starting at Chitkara and completing your degree at our global partner institution.
